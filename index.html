Independence Day

Independence Day is celebrated on August 14th to commemorate Pakistan's independence from British rule in 1947. It's a national holiday with various cultural events and flag hoisting ceremonies.

Allama Iqbal Day

Allama Iqbal Day is celebrated on November 9th to honor the birthday of Allama Iqbal, a renowned philosopher, poet, and politician. It's a day to celebrate his contributions to the nation.
